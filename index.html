Studia

School Management System

Studia is a comprehensive, sleek school management portal designed to centralize and streamline administrative, academic, and student-focused workflows. By integrating dynamic role-based access for administrators, teachers, and students, the platform acts as a unified digital ecosystem that simplifies everything from database management and resource organization to real-time communication. With its intuitive architecture and scalable design, Studia transforms complex school operations into a seamless, modern experience, powered by MGX Systems to ensure that educational institutions stay connected, organized, and efficiently managed.

ShopMonitor

Business Management System

ShopMonitor is an ERP Software project for SMEs that acts as a modern assistant and book-keeper. It automatically records the stock, the prices both CP & SP, the profits and losses, capital invested and overall worth. It also handles sales, calculates and records the debts incurred, expenses, generates receipts in thermal and A4 mode, provides overall summaries (daily, weekly, monthly etc) using figures, bars and charts! There's also user management where roles are assigned based on who's logged in. Meaning the business owner has total control over what the staff sees and does on their own portal.
